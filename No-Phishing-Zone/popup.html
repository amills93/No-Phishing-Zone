No-Phishing-Zone

Welcome to No-Phishing-Zone, we will help identify potentially malicious links and highlight them red for bad, orange for suspicious, and green for good. Refer to the risk rating for each email and URL. Ultimately, the choice to click on a link is yours, but we are here to help.

Refer to the Risk Ratings/Scores for descriptions. If the email or link is highlighted in purple the MAX API Calls for the day has been reached.

Email Risk Ratings:
  • 0 - invalid email address
  • 1 - dns valid, unreachable mail server
  • 2 - dns valid, temporary mail rejection error
  • 3 - dns valid, accepts all mail
  • 4 - dns valid, verified email exists
  • No Rating - Max API Calls per Day Hit

URL Risk Scores:
  • < 80 - Safe
  • >= 75 - Suspicious, usually due to patterns associated with malicious links.
  • >= 85 - high risk, strong confidence the URL is malicious.
  • 100 - Confirmed malware or phishing activity in the past 24-48 hours.
  • No Rating - Max API Calls per Day Hit
